Our Services

At Aquatech, we offer a wide range of services designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our water level controllers and automation systems are ideal for homes, businesses, and large industrial facilities.

Water Level Controllers

Our primary offering is the automatic water level controller that ensures your water tanks are never overfilled or run dry. It uses advanced sensors to detect the water levels in tanks and adjusts the supply of water accordingly, saving time and resources.

Installation and Maintenance

We provide professional installation services for all of our products, ensuring seamless integration with your water supply systems. Additionally, we offer maintenance packages that include regular checks and timely repairs to ensure optimal performance of your water management system.

Water Tank Automation

Our water tank automation systems not only monitor the water level but also automate the opening and closing of valves, allowing for remote management. This feature is ideal for larger properties or commercial buildings that require more control over their water management systems.

Consultation & Water Efficiency Audits

We provide consultation services to help you assess your water usage and identify areas for improvement. Our water efficiency audits will provide you with a detailed report and actionable recommendations to reduce water wastage and enhance operational efficiency.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated customer service team is available around the clock to assist with any issues you may face. Whether it's a technical problem or an emergency, we ensure a quick response to minimize downtime.
